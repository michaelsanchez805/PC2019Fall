Fall Power Coding Classes

Starting September 22nd at Millburn High School, join us for a coding adventure of a lifetime.

For one week only (June 24th to June 28th) at Millburn High School, join us for an incredible tour of coding, whether building websites, your own games, or iPhone apps.

Because of our high instructor to student ratio, your student will receive personalized instruction. Beginners and experts encouraged!

Questions? Contact me at mariacsanchez427@gmail.com or at 917-708-0884 and I'll be happy to help you out.

Learning to code is an adventure. And with Power Coding, an incredibly fun one.

Hi, I'm Maria Sanchez, and I'm a Sophomore at Millburn High School.

Over the past three years, I have helped at Cool Coding with my older brother John. Whatever course you choose, I guarantee it'll be an amazing ride!

I am super passionate about helping to give every kid the coding and STEM skills to succeed. In addition to Power Coding, I am working with friends to create STEMpowerment, a volunteer robotics and coding program that will be held at the Avon School in Newark this fall.

Fall Course Information

Dates and Prices

The fall courses are broken into 7 sessions, two hours each on Sunday mornings, from:

September September 22nd to November 17th (Skipping November 3rd and 10th)

Each week you can pick which time slot to go to 9:00am-10:30am or 11:00am-12:30pm.

We will be flexible and creative with make-ups if your child has scheduling challenges. At Millburn High School

  • The fall course fee is $200 for the 7 weeks, which includes a t-shirt, Rubix cube, laptop stickers and other fun prizes along the way.
  • All students MUST bring a computer and charger to camp each day to code with - any computer that can connect to the internet will work.
  • If you would like, you can sign up for two different tracks and attend both time slots for a discounted fee of $360

    • Available Tracks This Summer

    Select one of the following topics as your track for the:

    Web Development - Build your own websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in this fun course that's great for beginners.

    Game Development - Use JavaScript and p5.js to create fun and popular games like Pong and Flappy Bird, while learning essential programming concepts along the way including variables, conditional statements, and loops. Beginners welcome.

    Artificial Intelligence - A course for the Cool Coding veteran, or for interested students who want to move beyond the basics of coding into some advanced applications. ADD

    Little Coders (Grades 1-3) - Get exposure to coding concepts at an early age with this game-filled course that will keep everyone laughing. Students must know how to read.

    Our coding lessons are packed with fun games to teach concepts, endless laughs, and awesome projects that you'll build and be able to show your friends and family. Get ready to solve puzzles, win prizes, and have the adventure of a lifetime!
